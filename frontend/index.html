What you get

Everything in one place

Three input modes, smart sentence building, history, and analytics — all running locally without any subscription.

🎥
Real-Time Webcam
Captures frames at 100ms intervals. Stability system waits for 8 matching frames before confirming a letter, preventing false positives.
📤
Image Upload (Fixed)
Tries 6 different image sizes and preprocessing combinations to maximize detection. Works with any photo from phone or computer.
🎬
Video Upload
Analyzes one frame per second across the video. Deduplicates consecutive same letters to produce a clean sentence.
✍️
Sentence Builder
Letters build into words. Press Space to commit a word. Full sentence builds progressively. Copy to clipboard anytime.
💡
Word Suggestions
As you sign letters, matching word suggestions appear. Click to insert full words. Frequency-ranked — learns your vocabulary over time.
🗄️
Translation History
SQLite database stores every saved session. View history, statistics, most-used letters and words — all local, no internet.
Model performance

Nearly perfect accuracy

Random Forest trained on 87,000 images achieves 99.5% on the held-out test set. Most letters score above 98%.

Real-world webcam accuracy is slightly lower due to lighting variation and different backgrounds — this is called domain shift. The system compensates with CLAHE contrast enhancement and tuned detection thresholds.
